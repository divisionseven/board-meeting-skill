# Chairman's Final Meeting Synthesis Report

## Template 04 — Completed by: The Board Chairman

---

> **WHEN TO USE:** The Board Chairman fills this out in Phase 3, after reading
> all first and second session reports from all 10 board members. This is the
> final synthesis that produces the actionable recommendations.
>
> **COMPLETED BY:** The Board Chairman (solo, after all board sessions)
>
> **INSTRUCTIONS FOR THE BOARD CHAIRMAN:** You have received the root folder
> path. You MUST READ all session files before beginning:
>
> - All 10 members' First Session Response Reports (`session_1/*.md`)
> - All 10 members' Second Session Final Thoughts Reports (`session_2/*.md`)
>
> Your role is unlike any other agent in this meeting. You are not an advocate.
> You are not a member with a lens. You are the synthesis function. Your job is
> to read everything — every argument, every pushback, every convergence, every
> disagreement — and produce the most accurate, honest, and useful final picture
> of what the board has collectively determined.
>
> You are expected to:
>
> - Steelman every position before evaluating it
> - Give weight to arguments proportional to their quality, not their
>   popularity
> - Identify where multiple independent lenses reach the same conclusion
>   (strong signal)
> - Identify where lenses genuinely conflict and assess which argument is
>   stronger
> - Expose blind spots the entire board may share
> - Produce actionable, prioritized recommendations the calling agent can act
>   on
> - Be willing to reach a conclusion that no single board member advocated if
>   the evidence across all reports supports it
>
> Take your time. This document is the most important artifact of the entire
> meeting. Thoroughness is required. Speed is not.
>
> Write this report to `{root}/chairman_final_report.md`.
